# 🌌 Zenith — Premium Habit Tracker

Welcome to **Zenith**, a premium, high-performance, and beautifully designed web-based habit tracking application. Built with a focus on modern user experience (UX), micro-interactions, and visual data representation, Zenith helps users build consistency and track their daily habits effortlessly.

## ✨ Features

### 🎨 1. Advanced UI Architecture (Glassmorphism & Fluid Themes)
- **Fluid Dark/Light Modes:** Built-in seamless theme switching with calibrated custom properties (`:root` variables) for absolute comfort during day or night usage.
- **Micro-Interactions:** Custom transition curves (`cubic-bezier` spring dynamics) for buttons, inputs, and state changes.
- **Responsive Layout:** Designed from the ground up for desktop, tablet, and mobile browsers with overflow prevention and strict viewport scaling.

### 📈 2. Rich Data Analytics & Visualizations
- **Chart.js Integration:** Interactive graphs mapping your weekly performance and monthly consistency metrics.
- **Dynamic Stat Cards:** Real-time summary cards capturing your total habits, completion rate, current streak, and trends.
- **Circular Progress Rings:** High-fidelity SVG-based radial progress indicators to visualize achievement percentages.

### 🔥 3. Gamified Consistency & Habit Management
- **Streak Flame System:** Interactive animated fire indicators that scale with your daily consistency.
- **Interactive Calendar Grid:** Fully functional date-jumping matrices enabling history tracking and verification for custom date cells.
- **Category Pill Filtering:** Classify and isolate habits by categories (e.g., Fitness, Learning, Mental Health) on the fly.
- **Inline Custom Notes:** Attach contextual descriptions, reminders, or observations to individual habits.

---

## 💻 Tech Stack
- **Frontend:** HTML5 (Semantic Architecture), CSS3 (Custom Grid system, Flexbox, Variable Mapping)
- **Data Visualization:** Chart.js (External via CDN)
- **Typography:** Google Fonts integration (Clash Display, Cabinet Grotesk, Syne, DM Sans)
